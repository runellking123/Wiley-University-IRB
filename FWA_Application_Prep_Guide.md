# FWA Application Preparation Guide

## Wiley University - Federalwide Assurance (FWA) Application

**Form:** Federalwide Assurance (FWA) for the Protection of Human Subjects
**OMB No.:** 0990-0278
**Estimated completion time:** 30 minutes (first-time filers should expect longer)
**Submission:** Electronic only at [OHRP Electronic Filing System](https://ohrp.cit.nih.gov/efile/)

---

## Step 0: Create an Account

Before accessing the FWA form, create a submission account at https://ohrp.cit.nih.gov/efile/

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| Email Address | Must be institutional email that can receive from `OHRPElectronicSubmissionSystem@mail.nih.gov` |
| Password | 7-15 characters, at least one uppercase, one lowercase, one number or special character |

After submitting, OHRP issues a **Submission Number** valid for **30 days** to begin the application.

## Item 3 -- Statement of Ethical Principles

Select one:

| Option | Recommendation |
|--------|---------------|
| **The Belmont Report** | ✅ **Select this** (standard for U.S. universities) |
| Declaration of Helsinki | Typically for international/medical institutions |
| Other | Only if Wiley has its own institutional ethics statement |

---

## Item 4 -- Regulatory Compliance

Select one:

| Option | Recommendation |
|--------|---------------|
| Common Rule only (Subpart A) | Basic coverage only |
| **Common Rule + Subparts B, C, D, and E** | ✅ **Select this** -- broadest protections |

**Subparts explained:**
- **Subpart A (Common Rule):** Basic federal policy for protection of human subjects
- **Subpart B:** Additional protections for pregnant women, fetuses, and neonates
- **Subpart C:** Additional protections for prisoners
- **Subpart D:** Additional protections for children
- **Subpart E:** Registration of IRBs

---

## Item 5 -- Voluntary Extension

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| 5(a) | Pre-printed — automatically applies to federally funded research |
| **5(b) (optional)** | ✅ **Recommended** — Check this to apply protections to ALL research (not just federally funded). Simplifies compliance and demonstrates comprehensive commitment. |

> **Decision needed:** Discuss with institutional leadership whether to extend protections to all research regardless of funding source.

---

## Item 6 -- IRB Designation (Critical Decision)

### ⬜ Decision: Internal IRB or External IRB?

#### Option A: Establish an Internal IRB

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Minimum 5 members | Per 45 CFR 46.107 |
| At least 1 scientist | Member with scientific expertise |
| At least 1 non-scientist | Member whose primary concerns are non-scientific |
| At least 1 unaffiliated member | Not otherwise affiliated with Wiley |
| Diversity | Varied backgrounds, including race, gender, cultural considerations |
| Register IRB with OHRP | Must be done **BEFORE** filing the FWA |
| Receive IRB Registration Number | Format: IRB00XXXXXX |

#### Option B: Rely on an External IRB

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Identify an external IRB | Nearby university IRB or commercial IRB (e.g., Advarra, WCG IRB) |
| Execute an IRB Authorization Agreement | Formal reliance agreement between institutions |
| Obtain their OHRP Registration Number | Format: IRB00XXXXXX |

#### For each designated IRB, you will provide:

| Field | Info Needed |
|-------|-------------|
| IRB Organization Name | Name of the organization operating the IRB |
| OHRP IRB Registration Number | 8-digit number (IRB00XXXXXX) |
| Internal or External | Whether operated by Wiley or another organization |

## Pre-Application Checklist

### Institutional Decisions
- [ ] Confirm exact legal name of institution (as on charter documents)
- [ ] List all institutional components operating under different names
- [ ] Select ethical principles (recommend: The Belmont Report)
- [ ] Select regulatory scope (recommend: Common Rule + Subparts B, C, D, E)
- [ ] Decide on voluntary extension to all research (Item 5b)
- [ ] **Decide: internal IRB or external IRB**

### If Internal IRB
- [ ] Recruit at least 5 IRB members meeting 45 CFR 46.107 requirements
- [ ] Register the IRB with OHRP separately (before filing the FWA)
- [ ] Receive OHRP IRB Registration Number (IRB00XXXXXX)

### If External IRB
- [ ] Identify and contact the external IRB
- [ ] Execute an IRB Authorization Agreement / Reliance Agreement
- [ ] Obtain their OHRP IRB Registration Number (IRB00XXXXXX)

### Personnel Information
- [ ] Identify Human Protections Administrator and collect all contact info
- [ ] Collect Signatory Official (Dr. Felton) fax number and email
- [ ] Dr. Felton reads the [Terms of the FWA](https://www.hhs.gov/ohrp/register-irbs-and-obtain-fwas/fwas/fwa-protection-of-human-subjecct/index.html)

### Technical
- [ ] Verify institutional email can receive from `OHRPElectronicSubmissionSystem@mail.nih.gov`
- [ ] Access [OHRP Electronic Filing System](https://ohrp.cit.nih.gov/efile/) and create account

## Post-Submission Notes

- FWA is **valid for 5 years** and must be renewed
- Must **update FWA within 90 days** if changes occur to: legal name, HPA, or Signatory Official
- Once approved, the submitter, HPA, and Signatory Official all receive email notification
- If unable to submit electronically, alternatives:
  - **Fax:** (240) 453-6909
  - **Email:** IRBorFWA@hhs.gov
  - **Mail:** Division of Policy and Assurances, OHRP, 1101 Wootton Parkway, Suite 200, Rockville, MD 20852
